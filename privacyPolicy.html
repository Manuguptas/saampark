LEGAL / PRIVACY NOTICE

SCS Group Privacy Notice

Last Updated: 8 August 2025

1. Introduction

SCS Group (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

We provide services including Logo & Graphic Design, Digital Marketing, Loan Services, Insurance Services, Education Consultancy, and Legal Services. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you engage with our services.

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

We process personal data based on lawful grounds such as consent, contractual necessity, legal obligation, and legitimate interest.

  • To provide requested services and manage client relationships.
  • To process loan, insurance, and legal documentation.
  • To manage digital marketing campaigns and graphic design services.
  • To improve website performance and service quality.
  • To communicate important updates and customer support responses.

3. Contractual and Regulatory Compliance

We process personal data to administer contracts, verify identity, maintain financial records, and comply with applicable regulatory and statutory obligations.

4. Sharing of Personal Data

Why we share data: To deliver services effectively, comply with legal requirements, and protect business interests.

Third-party service providers may include:

  • Financial institutions and payment processors
  • Insurance partners
  • Legal consultants and compliance advisors
  • Marketing and hosting service providers
  • IT and cloud infrastructure providers

Where SCS Group operates with affiliated entities, personal data may be shared internally for administrative, compliance, and operational purposes. All third parties are required to implement appropriate data protection safeguards.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk. This includes encryption, secure servers, access controls, and routine system monitoring to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice, including compliance with legal, accounting, and reporting requirements. Data that is no longer required will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Where processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing conducted prior to withdrawal. Upon receiving your request, we will cease processing unless we are legally required to retain certain information.
